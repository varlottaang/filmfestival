Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival

5th-8th August 2017

"Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival" just got the approval to show movies at Brooklyn Bridge Park this year from August 5th through 8th, from 6pm to midnight. We can't wait to help the public discover awesome films! Shows will be free and open to everyone, make sure to reserve your seat!

Everyone will receive free beer (or coke) and french fries (or pop corn) after 10 p.m.

Every night there will be speacial guests. Don't miss the show, our Special Guest List is insane! Just few names:

Bradley Cooper, Emma Stone, Sylvester Stallone, Quentin Tarantino, U2 ...

Batman Begins is a 2005 superhero film based on the DC Comics character Batman, directed by Christopher Nolan and written by Nolan and David S. Goyer.
